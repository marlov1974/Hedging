# P0040 Source Research

## Public terminology checked

- Euronext/Nord Pool public background confirms the Nordic power market context and that Euronext has acquired Nasdaq's Nordic power futures business.
- Public EPAD terminology describes Electricity Price Area Differentials as contracts used to hedge area price risk between an area price and the system price.

## Naming decision

The implementation uses conservative synthetic names:

```text
Nordic Electricity Base Load Future Month 2027-01
Nordic Electricity EPAD SE3 Month 2027-01
```

The names intentionally avoid real exchange product codes, trade IDs or counterparty identifiers.
